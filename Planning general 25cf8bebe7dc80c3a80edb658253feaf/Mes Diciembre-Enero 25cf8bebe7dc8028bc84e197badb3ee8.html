📘 Month 4 – Grammar & Topics

Week 13: Third Conditional + Mixed Conditionals

🔹 Grammar Focus

  • Third Conditional (imaginary past situations)

    → If I had studied harder, I would have passed the exam.

  • Mixed Conditionals
    • Past → present: If I had studied, I would be more confident now.
    • Present → past: If I were taller, I could have played basketball professionally.

🔗 Resources

  • Cambridge: Third conditional
  • British Council: Third conditional
  • Exercises: Perfect English Grammar – Third & Mixed

Week 14: Inversion for Emphasis

🔹 Grammar Focus

  • Inversion = change word order for emphasis (formal style).
  • Examples:
    • Never have I seen such a beautiful view.
    • Only then did I realize my mistake.
    • Not until she left did I understand.
  • Common with: never, rarely, hardly, not only, scarcely, no sooner…

🔗 Resources

  • Cambridge: Inversion
  • British Council: Negative adverbials and inversion
  • Exercises: Perfect English Grammar – Inversion

Week 15: Linking Words (connectors for contrast, cause, result, concession)

🔹 Grammar Focus

  • Contrast: although, though, even though, whereas, while.
  • Cause & effect: because of, due to, therefore, as a result.
  • Concession: despite, in spite of.
  • Addition: moreover, in addition, furthermore.

🔗 Resources

  • Cambridge: Linking words
  • British Council: Linking words
